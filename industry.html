Apple Inc. stands as a global leader in technology, innovation, and industrial design, shaping the future of consumer electronics. With over 2 billion active devices worldwide, Apple’s impact extends across smartphones, laptops, wearables, and cloud services. The company's $3 trillion market valuation cements its dominance in the industry, while its 500+ Apple Stores in 25+ countries redefine retail experiences. From the groundbreaking iPhone revolution in 2007 to the latest M-series chips powering MacBooks, Apple continues to push the boundaries of hardware and software integration. Its supply chain, spanning China, India, the U.S., and Vietnam, delivers cutting-edge products while prioritizing sustainability, aiming for carbon neutrality by 2030. Through seamless innovation and infrastructure, Apple’s commitment to excellence remains unmatched in the tech industry.
